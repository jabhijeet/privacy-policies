📋1. Information We Collect

1.1 Financial Data You Provide

  • Investment Holdings: Asset names, quantities, purchase prices, dates, and categories (stocks, mutual funds, crypto, fixed deposits, etc.) that you manually enter or import.
  • Transaction Records: Buy/sell transactions, dividend payments, interest credits, and systematic investment plan details.
  • Document Uploads: When you use the AI Document Parser, we process images (photos/scans) or text extracted from your financial statements to extract investment details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Anonymized, aggregated information about how you interact with the App (e.g., features used, screen views, session duration). This data is stored locally only and is not transmitted to external analytics servers.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for debugging and compatibility purposes.

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal Identifiers: We do not require or collect your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • Bank Credentials: WealthLens does not connect to your bank or brokerage accounts. We never see or store your banking usernames or passwords.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise geolocation data.
  • Biometric Data: If you enable biometric lock (fingerprint/face), the authentication is handled entirely by your device's operating system. We never receive or store your biometric data.

⚙️2. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: To calculate portfolio value, asset allocation, performance metrics (XIRR, CAGR), and generate financial summaries on your device.
  • AI-Powered Insights: To provide intelligent portfolio analysis and document parsing using Large Language Models (LLMs). When you use these features, only relevant, anonymized portfolio summaries or document text are sent to the LLM provider.
  • Market Data: To fetch current asset prices from public providers (e.g., Yahoo Finance). Only asset symbols are shared to retrieve valuations.
  • Notifications: To send local push notifications for price alerts, maturity reminders, premium due dates, and other events you configure.
  • Security: To encrypt your data, authenticate your identity via biometric lock, and protect against unauthorized access.
  • Improvement: To identify bugs, crashes, and performance issues so we can improve the App.

🔒3. Data Storage and Security

Local-First Architecture

WealthLens is designed as a local-first application. All your sensitive financial data — holdings, transactions, and account details — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not maintain remote databases or cloud backups of your personal portfolio data.

Encryption

  • At Rest: Your local database is encrypted using AES-256 via SQLCipher. All data written to disk is fully encrypted.
  • Key Management: The 256-bit encryption key is unique to your device and stored in your system's hardware-backed Secure Enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Backups: Exported backup files are AES-256 encrypted. You control the encryption password.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You can wipe all data at any time using the "Delete All Data" option in the App's Settings. This permanently deletes the database and associated encryption keys from your device.

🔗4. Third-Party Services & Disclosures

4.1 LLM Providers (AI Features)

When you use AI-powered features (Insights, Document Parsing), data is sent to third-party LLM providers. The following providers are supported:

  • OpenAI (GPT models) — Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude models) — Privacy Policy
  • Google (Gemini models) — Privacy Policy

You choose which provider to use and provide your own API key. Data sent to these providers is subject to their respective privacy policies and data usage terms. We recommend reviewing each provider's policy. Only anonymized portfolio summaries or document text you explicitly submit are transmitted.

4.2 Market Data Providers

  • Yahoo Finance: Asset symbols are sent to retrieve current market prices.
  • CoinGecko: Cryptocurrency symbols are sent to retrieve current crypto prices.
  • Frankfurter API: Currency codes are sent to retrieve foreign exchange rates.

4.3 Legal Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency). Since your data is stored locally, we have limited ability to produce it.

⚖️5. Your Data Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: View all your data within the App and export encrypted backups in JSON format for your own records.
  • Deletion (Right to Erasure): Permanently delete all data from your device using the "Delete All Data" option in Settings. This action is irreversible.
  • Opt-Out of AI Features: Disable AI-powered insights and document parsing at any time in Settings.
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: Manage notification preferences individually in Settings.
  • Biometric Lock: Enable or disable biometric authentication in Settings.
  • Privacy Mode: Enable Privacy Mode to mask sensitive financial amounts on screen.

To exercise any of these rights, use the options within the App's Settings screen. Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full and immediate control.
