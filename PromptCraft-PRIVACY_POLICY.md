# Privacy Policy for PromptCraft

**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

PromptCraft ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Prompt Templates & Queries**: The text of your custom prompts, queries, and any input you submit to the App.
- **Query Results**: Responses generated by AI models based on your queries.
- **API Keys**: If you configure LLM providers (e.g., OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic), you may enter API keys, which are stored securely on your device.
- **Settings and Provider Configuration**: Settings such as selected LLM provider, theme, provider metadata, and other configuration options are stored securely on your device.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: The App may log usage statistics (e.g., frequency of feature use) to improve functionality. This data is stored locally and is not transmitted to us.
- **Device Information**: The App may collect anonymous device information (e.g., operating system version) for compatibility purposes.

### 1.3 Information from Third‑Party Services
When you use the App to interact with external LLM providers (OpenAI, Google Gemini, Anthropic, etc.), your queries and any attached files are transmitted to those providers according to their respective privacy policies. We do not control how these third parties handle your data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:
- To provide and maintain the App’s core functionality (prompt management, AI query execution, history tracking).
- To store your settings, provider configuration, and API keys locally so you do not need to re‑enter them.
- To improve the App’s performance and user experience.
- To enable vector search (semantic similarity) on your past results, which operates entirely on your device.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
All your prompts, query history, results, and embeddings are stored **locally** on your device in an encrypted SQLite database. No personal data is automatically uploaded to our servers.

### 3.2 Secure Storage of Settings and Sensitive Data
App settings, provider configuration, API keys, authentication tokens, and local response cache entries are stored using platform‑specific secure storage mechanisms where available and are never transmitted to us.

### 3.3 Data Retention
You can delete your data at any time through the App’s interface (e.g., clearing history, deleting prompts). Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

## 4. Data Sharing and Third‑Party Disclosures

**We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to outside parties.** However, the following limited sharing occurs:

- **Third‑Party LLM Providers**: When you submit a query, the query text and any attached files are sent to the LLM provider you have selected (e.g., OpenAI, Google, Anthropic). This transmission is necessary to obtain AI responses. Each provider’s own privacy policy governs how they handle your data.
- **Service Providers**: We may engage trusted third‑party services (such as analytics or crash‑reporting tools) that collect anonymous, aggregated data to help us improve the App. These services cannot identify you personally.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access and Correction**: You can view, edit, or delete your prompts, query history, and results directly within the App.
- **API Keys**: You can remove or update your API keys at any time in the LLM Providers settings.
- **Opt‑out of Analytics**: If the App includes analytics, you can opt‑out in the Settings screen.
- **Data Export**: You may export your prompts and results via the App’s export functionality (if available).

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
