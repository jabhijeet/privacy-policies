# Privacy Policy for LittleMind AI

**Last Updated: May 15, 2026**

Welcome to LittleMind AI. Your privacy and your child's safety are our top priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our application.

## 1. Introduction
LittleMind AI is an AI-powered story and chat companion designed for children. We are committed to providing a safe, private, and secure environment for children to explore their creativity.

## 2. Information Collection
LittleMind AI is designed with privacy in mind:
- **No Accounts Required**: We do not require you to create a user account to use the application.
- **Local Personalization**: Information such as your child's name, age, and interests is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device except when sent as context for AI responses.
- **No Personal Data Harvesting**: We do not sell or trade your child's information to third parties.

## 3. AI Processing & Data Handling
When you or your child interacts with LittleMind AI:
- **Audio to Text**: Speech is converted to text locally or via system services.
- **AI Providers**: The text, along with locally stored personalization data, is sent to the AI provider you have configured (e.g., Google Gemini, Ollama, OpenRouter, etc.).
- **No Server Storage**: We do not store your conversations on our own servers. 
- **Local History**: Your chat history is saved locally on your device for your reference and can be cleared at any time.

## 4. Security & Encryption
- **Data Protection**: We use industry-standard encryption to protect locally stored data, including API keys and chat history.
- **API Keys**: All configured API keys are stored securely on your device.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The app allows connection to various AI providers. Please review the privacy policies of the specific providers you choose to use:
- Google Gemini
- OpenRouter
- Hugging Face
- Ollama (Local)

## 6. Parental Controls
LittleMind AI includes features specifically for parents to guide the experience, including response mode selection and history review. We encourage parents to be involved in their child's interactions with AI.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out via our project repository.
